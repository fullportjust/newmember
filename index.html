Phase 03 — The core skill

How our trades work.

Every signal we post uses the same four tags. Learn these and you can follow any call without second-guessing.

Video · Reading a signal: LOAD / BUY / TRIM / SELL
[LOAD]

Get the contract ready on your broker — but don't buy yet. You're prepping so you can move the moment the buy comes.

[BUY]

Enter the position. This is the green light to open the trade at the called contract.

[TRIM]

Sell a portion — usually half or more — to bank profit. If you only hold one contract, this is your sell.

[SELL]

Close the rest. The position is done and we're out.

The golden rule: trim into strength, then move your stop to breakeven so a winning trade can't turn into a loss. Most calls signal [SELL] well before any hard stop.

Phase 04 — Non-negotiable

Risk & portfolio management.

This is the most important section on the page. Good entries don't keep you in the game — sizing and risk do. Most traders don't lose because they're wrong; they lose because they're too big when they're wrong.

Video · Sizing, risk & protecting your portfolio
Size every trade as a % of your portfolio — and assume it can go to zero. Options expire worthless all the time. If a full loss on a position would hurt, you're too big.

Position sizing

  • Full — 3–5% of your portfolio (default)
  • Small — 1–3% when conviction is lower
  • Lotto — under 1%, high risk, can go to zero
  • Smaller accounts scale the % up sensibly — don't chase near-worthless contracts

Risk by time to expiration

  • 1–7 days — ultra high risk (theta, IV crush)
  • 8–14 days — moderate-high; consider spreads
  • 15–30 days — moderate, more forgiving
  • 30+ days — lower risk; scaling & LEAPS
Protect what you make. Trim into strength, move your stop to breakeven, and never let a green trade turn red. Survival first — the gains compound when you're still here next month.

Before you trade

The non-negotiables.

Break these and the system stops working. They're not optional.

✗No risk plan, no trade.

Know your entry, your trim, and your stop before you click buy.

✗Never size what you can't lose.

Options can go to zero. Size every position so a full loss won't hurt you.

✗Admins never DM you first.

Any "copy trader" or investment DM is a scam. Verify names inside the server only.
